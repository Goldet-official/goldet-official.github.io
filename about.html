About Goldet

Goldet is a golden-themed, stylish website inspired by the look & feel of BlooketWarrior's site — but redesigned and renamed for your own brand. It focuses on bold metallic accents, high-contrast dark backgrounds, and clean, responsive layouts.

Design

Gold accents, subtle glows, and a dark canvas for maximum contrast. Components are designed to be reusable and accessible.

Performance

Minimal external dependencies — these pages are lightweight and optimized for quick loads.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive grid, touch-friendly controls, and readable text sizes across devices.

Customizable

Color variables are inlined in each file — change a few hex values and the whole theme updates.
